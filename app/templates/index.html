So, what does this site do? Mainly, two things.

  1. Select your keepers and tag Super Franchise, Franchise and Transition players
  2. Conduct the RFA bidding process one all the appropriate players have been kept/tagged

So, its pretty self explanitory. Go to the keepers tab and select your keepers and tag your players. If you screw up or want to reconsider, you will be able to do that up until the cutoff date for selecting keepers. Rule changes and updates were sent out in the June Newsletter. Go there if you have any questions about the rule changes.

On the Keepers page if you have a keeper from last year, his salary is updated and listed as a K1. All other salaries listed are the players salaries at the end of the season last year. If you want to keep a player, the salary this year is $5 more than whats listed, and next year another $5.
